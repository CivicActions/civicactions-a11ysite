# CivicActions' Accessibility Champions Program

Building accessibility expertise and advocacy across our organization through structured mentorship and community building.

## About the Program

The CivicActions Accessibility Champions program develops internal accessibility expertise by training team members to become accessibility advocates and resources within their practice areas and project teams.

## Program Goals

### Individual Development
- Build practical accessibility skills
- Develop confidence in accessibility advocacy
- Create career growth opportunities in accessibility
- Connect champions with the broader accessibility community

### Organizational Impact
- Embed accessibility expertise across practice areas
- Improve accessibility outcomes on client projects
- Build a culture of inclusive design
- Share knowledge across the organization

## How It Works

### Champion Selection
Champions are nominated by their peers or self-nominate based on:
- Interest in accessibility and inclusive design
- Commitment to learning and sharing knowledge
- Position to influence accessibility practices in their role
- Availability to participate in program activities

### Training and Development
- Monthly accessibility skill-building sessions
- Hands-on project work with mentorship
- Conference attendance and community engagement
- Cross-functional collaboration opportunities

### Ongoing Support
- Regular check-ins with accessibility practice leads
- Access to training resources and tools
- Connection with external accessibility communities
- Recognition for accessibility contributions

## Current Champions

Our champions represent diverse roles across CivicActions, including developers, designers, project managers, and content strategists.

## Joining the Program

Interested in becoming an accessibility champion? Learn about our application process and current opportunities.
