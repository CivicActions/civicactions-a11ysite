# AT Banter Podcast

CivicActions accessibility experts join the AT Banter podcast to discuss assistive technology, inclusive design, and the future of digital accessibility.

## About AT Banter

AT Banter is a podcast dedicated to discussing assistive technology, accessibility, and inclusive design from the perspectives of users, advocates, and industry professionals.

### Episode Featuring CivicActions

In this episode, our accessibility team discusses:

- **Government accessibility requirements** and how they impact private sector innovation
- **Open source accessibility tools** and their role in democratizing inclusive design
- **Real-world implementation challenges** and practical solutions for development teams
- **Community building** around accessibility practices and knowledge sharing

### Key Topics Covered

#### Assistive Technology Landscape
- Current state of screen readers, voice recognition, and alternative input devices
- Emerging technologies and their potential impact on accessibility
- Integration challenges with modern web frameworks and applications

#### Government Accessibility Leadership
- Section 508 compliance as a driver for broader accessibility adoption
- Procurement requirements and their influence on vendor accessibility priorities
- Cross-agency collaboration on accessibility standards and implementation

#### Open Source Accessibility
- Benefits of open source tools for accessibility testing and development
- Community-driven solutions versus proprietary accessibility software
- Contributing to accessibility projects and building expertise
